Support for PicklingÂ¶

The following PyQt5 classes may be pickled.

  • QByteArray
  • QColor
  • QDate
  • QDateTime
  • QKeySequence
  • QLine
  • QLineF
  • QPoint
  • QPointF
  • QPolygon
  • QRect
  • QRectF
  • QSize
  • QSizeF
  • QTime

Also all named enums (PyQt5.QtCore.Qt.Key for example) may be pickled.
